About us
Maker Bhavan As Academic Makerspace and More
Engineering education around the world is undergoing a radical shift with many new ideas, pedagogies and teaching methods being employed to enhance student learning and performance. Prominent among these is “active learning”, - a strategic, active engagement of students in opportunities to learn by doing, with a goal to empower them to apply their knowledge in practical endeavours in the real world. IIT Gandhinagar (IITGN), which has been at the forefront of many innovative ideas in higher education, aims to pioneer the learning-by-doing approach in India, thereby leading the charge in yet another sphere of engineering education.

Maker Bhavan is working towards embedding active learning as a core value in the educational experience of students across all IITGN disciplines and programs. One of the central goals is to foster experiential learning, design thinking, creativity and innovation among students. This includes not just learning-by-doing, but also doing-before-learning, and encompassing engineering, science, mathematics, biomedical, humanities, entrepreneurship and many more domains. Our work is to encourage a culture of student autonomy, empowerment, and to enable all students in the full capacity, to achieve customized skill development and competence so that they become innovative thinkers and doers in their personal and professional life and in their community.

Mission, vision and goals of Maker Bhavan are in line with IITGN’s overall mission

In the Media
Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar (IITGN) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to establish ‘Maker Bhavan’ at the Institute. Maker Bhavan will promote an open and informal learning environment that encourages collaborative learning among students through hands-on experimentation. ‘Maker Bhavan’ space will have team work areas, fabrication area, machine shop, creativity studio, and electronics labs with all necessary tools and equipment.
