Laser cutter - GCC X252 80 watt
The LaserPro 252X is a laser cutting and engraving machine, equipped with a dependable sealed CO2 laser.
Specifications
  • Mode: X252RX
  • Laser Source: 80W, Sealed CO2 Laser
  • Laser Cooling: Water Cooled
  • Working Area: 25 in. x18 in. (635 x 458 mm)
  • Table Size: 31.1 x 20.9 in. (790 x 530 mm)
  • Driving Motor: Closed-loop DC servo control
  • Engraving capability: 256-level gray scale image processing capability.
  • Resolution (DPI): 125, 250, 500, 1000
  • Z-Axis Movement: Automatic
  • Focus Lens: Standard 2.0"
  • Safety Class I Laser Product Compliant with EN60825
  • Class II Laser Product Compliant with CDRH
  • 2006/42/EC Machinery Directive Compliance
  • Class 4 Laser Product Compliant with CDRH with the optional pass-through door module
  • Power: Operation Voltage 200-240VAC, 50/60Hz Auto Switching, MAX. 8A
  • Accessories: Water Chiller, Air Pump
