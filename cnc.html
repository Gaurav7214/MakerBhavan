Compact CNC Robokits (600*400 Robokits)
This is a desktop milling machine for non metal cutting and milling for 2D and 3D surface creation.
Specifications
  • Machine Dimensions: 810×610×395mm
  • Working Area:590×390mm
  • Z-axis Stroke:70mm, (Engraving depth depends on the length of the tool edge)
  • Maximum job height: <=105mm
  • Repeat positioning Accuracy: 0.05mm
  • Materials: Engraving & Cutting for Wood,bamboo,PCB,PVC,PMMA,plastics,ABS etc.
  • Spindle: 1.5Kw ER11 Air Cooled 80mm 220V 24000rpm
