Roland SG540 or SG640 [Vinyl cutting and printing machine]
Advanced trueVIS SG2 series large format printer/cutter, which has the superior color and cutting features.
Specifications
  • Printing method: Piezo ink-jet
  • Width: 10.8 to 54 in. (275 to 1,371mm)
  • Thickness: Max. 39 mil (1.0 mm) with liner, for printing
  • Max. 16 mil (0.4 mm) with liner, and 9mil (0.22 mm) without liner, for cutting
  • Roll outer diameter: Max. 8.3 in. (210 mm)
  • Roll weight: Max. 30 kg
  • Core diameter: 3 in. (76.2 mm) or 2in. (50.8 mm)
  • Printing/cutting width: Max. 53 in. (1,346 mm)
  • Printing resolution: Max. 1,200 dpi
  • Cutting speed: 0.4 to 11.8 in/s (10 to 300 mm/s)
  • Blade force: 30 to 500gf
  • Blade Type: Roland CAMM-1 series
  • Blade Offset: 0.0 to 59.1 mil (0.000 to 1.500 mm)
